About VedantPay

Vedantpay : A New face of Banking Convenience

Taking banking to the people With its unique technology-driven focus, it is on course to build one of India’s largest networks of last-mile retail outlets to deliver banking, e-governance services.Bridge the Gap and Connect to Banking Ecosystem. Our motto is to distribution of financial services across the length and breadth of state.

Through our safe, secure, reliable and quickest payment gateway we believe to connect Semi-urban & Rural India to the mainstream of economic development.
