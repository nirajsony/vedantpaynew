About VedantPay

Vedantpay : A New face of Banking Convenience

Vedant Pay A unit of Vedant Asset Pvt.Ltd as one of the largest Business Correspondent Network in India has established 500+ Kiosks (Customer Service Point: a kind of Information technology enabled kiosk run by local entrepreneur for facilitating basic banking services) in 28 states of India with an special focus on rural areas.

Vedant Pay is known for its best services to rural unbanked people considering banking services. This is possible with a well evolved in-house IT architecture with Integrated MIS with usage of quality human resource. This mechanism delivers better fund management systems and smooth operations across country, continuous monitoring along with initial hand holding support with very quick grievances resolution mechanism provides additional strength for catering to the financial inclusion needs of the country.


Who We Are ( हम कौन हैं )

Vedant Pay empowers service providers to serve their end customers most efficiently by enabling entities involved in accepting payments like retail merchants, professionals, schools etc. to accept payments through multiple digital channels. It also provides a mechanism of achieving increased revenue numbers for these entities through customized Value-Added Services..

What We Do ( हम क्या करते हैं )

Vedant Pay has a transformative potential with focus on delivering Banking and Financial Services at your doorstep in urban and rural areas. We are committed to develop a strong partner based relationship offering AEPS, DMT, Insurance, Mutual Funds, BBPS, M-POS along with other services like PAN Card, E-ticketing etc.

Our Future Plans ( हमारी भविष्य की योजनाएं )

To further boost digital transactions, Vedant Pay is making constant push to digitization of financial services through necessary education, digital rewards and incentives along with intelligent innovations in financial transactions focused on delivering last mile financial connectivity and improving customer experience.
