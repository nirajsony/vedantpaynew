Aadhaar Enabled Payment System

Aadhaar Enabled Payment System or AEPS is a new payment service offered by the National Payments Corporation of India to banks and financial institutions using ‘Aadhaar’. Aadhaar is a unique identification number issued by Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). This service can be availed at Customer Access Points (CSPs) managed by Business Correspondents (BCs) using Point of Sale (POS) devices. Oxigen, being one such financial institution, provides AEPS services across India through its appointed BCs who use Oxigen Micro ATM PoS machines.


A Business Correspondent who is an intermediary appointed by a bank to extend Banking services in areas where they do not have a branch can own an Oxigen Micro ATM to offer AEPS services to bank customers. The scope of activities undertaken by the Business Correspondents includes, inter alia, collection of small value deposits, cash withdrawals and small value interbank remittances.
